About Us

The maddest doner joint in town — and proud of it.

Born From a Craving

It all started with a simple, stubborn idea: nobody was making a doner worth obsessing over. Overpriced? Yes. Underseasoned? Always. Sad, soggy bread? Don't even get us started.

So we fired up the skewer, perfected the spice blend after 47 failed attempts (yes, forty-seven), and DonkeyDoner was born — loud, unapologetic, and absolutely delicious.

The donkey in our name? Stubborn dedication. We don't cut corners, we don't rush the meat, and we will never, ever give you a cold doner.

🌯 Fun Facts

  • The word "doner" literally means "rotating" in Turkish — named after the legendary spinning skewer that makes it all possible.
  • Germans eat roughly 400 million doners per year. That's more than any other country on Earth. We're taking notes.
  • Our skewer never stops spinning from open to close. If it stops, something has gone very, very wrong.
  • The perfect doner-to-bread ratio took us three months to nail. We ate a lot of imperfect ones along the way. No regrets.
  • Our secret sauce has 9 ingredients. We know the 10th one — it's the care we put into making it every single morning.
